## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no account required. You enter your tools, you get your audit. Email is optional and only asked after you've seen your results.

**Q: How does Credex make money from this?**
A: For teams with significant savings (>$500/mo), Credex offers discounted AI credits sourced from companies that overforecast. If that's relevant to you, we'll let you know. If not, we won't bother you.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: We verify every price from official vendor pages each week. All sources are cited in our pricing documentation. AI tool pricing changes frequently — if you see an error, email us.

**Q: Does this work for API usage, not just subscriptions?**
A: Yes. We support Anthropic API direct and OpenAI API direct as separate entries. Enter your average monthly spend and we'll analyze whether a flat subscription makes more sense at your usage level.

**Q: Will you spam me?**
A: No. You get one email with your report. If your savings are significant, a Credex advisor may reach out once. You can unsubscribe at any time.
