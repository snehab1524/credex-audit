# Unit Economics

## What Is a Converted Lead Worth to Credex?

A startup buying discounted AI credits through Credex:
- Typical first purchase: $2,000–$5,000 in credits
- Credex margin: ~15–25% on credits (estimated, based on typical reseller economics)
- Revenue per converted customer: **$300–$1,250** on first purchase
- With 2 purchases/year average: **$600–$2,500 LTV per customer (year 1)**

Using a conservative $500 revenue per converted customer for math below.

## CAC by Channel

| Channel | Estimated CAC | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Show HN / organic | ~$0 | Time cost only, not cash |
| Founder Slack DMs | ~$5 | Time cost amortized |
| Twitter organic | ~$0–$10 | Thread creation time |
| Referral (share link) | ~$0 | Viral loop built into product |
| Paid (future) | $80–$150 | Estimated for B2B SaaS tool |

At $0–$10 organic CAC vs $500 LTV: **unit economics are strong if conversion holds**.

## Conversion Funnel

| Stage | Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Landing page → audit completed | 40% | High intent, low friction (no login) |
| Audit completed → email captured | 20% | Post-value gate, industry standard |
| Email captured → consultation booked | 10% | Only high-savings cases get hard CTA |
| Consultation booked → credit purchase | 40% | Warm lead, specific savings already shown |

**End-to-end:** 100 visitors → 40 audits → 8 emails → 0.8 consultations → 0.32 purchases

At 100 visitors → 0.32 purchases → $160 revenue
To break even on 1 hour of content creation ($50 equivalent): need ~313 visitors per piece of content.
A decent Show HN drives 300–500 visitors → **breakeven on day 1 of distribution.**

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

**Revenue target:** $1,000,000 ARR = ~$83,333/month

At $500 revenue per customer and one purchase/year:
- Need **167 new customers/month** at steady state

Working backward from funnel:
- 167 purchases/mo ÷ 40% close rate = 418 consultations/mo
- 418 consultations ÷ 10% book rate = 4,180 email captures/mo
- 4,180 emails ÷ 20% capture rate = 20,900 audit completions/mo
- 20,900 audits ÷ 40% completion rate = **52,250 visitors/month needed**

**What has to be true:**
1. The tool achieves organic distribution at ~1,700 visitors/day — achievable with consistent content + SEO + referral loop, but requires 6+ months of compounding
2. Credex's existing customer relationships convert to tool users (est. 500 existing contacts → 200 audits → 40 emails → 4 consultations in month 1)
3. Credit purchase LTV is $500+ (i.e., customers buy more than once or refer others)

**Most likely failure mode:** Conversion from audit → consultation is lower than 10% because most users have low savings. Mitigation: only send hard Credex CTA for >$500/mo savings cases. Be honest for low-savings cases — this builds trust and referral rate.

**Most likely upside driver:** The shareable URL is genuinely viral in founder Slack groups. One post by the right account → 500 audits in a day. This happened with tools like Exploding Topics, Nomad List, and similar founder-targeted free utilities.
